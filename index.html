Project Idea

With the hustle and bustle of home automation a potential project idea I have stems from home automation, as an avid motorcyclist after coming home for a long ride there is always that awkward moment when approaching the garage door just to fumble around the pockets to find the remote. My project idea is to implement a device or devices that work together to safely open the garage door allowing access without the need for a separate remote, I believe it will make a significant impact of motorcyclists or motorists alike.

The motivation for this project lies in the fact that most households will have some sort of vehicle, In 1996 the world was home to roughly around 670 Million vehicles and growing. Source: https://www.carsguide.com.au/car-advice/how-many-cars-are-there-in-the-world-70629 although a more favoured personal project of mine I think if it ever comes to fruition it would serve greatly with ease as to how it could conveniently remove a manual process in one’s life. My idea lies in utilising some existing products but combining them into a all in one project (Raspberry Pi, infrared sensors, geofencing or Bluetooth activation) with home automation in style and trending I am very certain that this project may be of interests to others.

The idea behind this project is that, it is simply a smart garage opener, however with a more comprehensive package, an all in one solution it can detect a user in range via geofencing or wireless network detection activation or even Bluetooth, that’s the activation trigger side of things, now on to the safety aspect, being automated some safety measures must be in place to maintain safety and well being to those around us, should the garage opener detect an obstacle or vehicle present it will carry out certain functions that are all programmable. On top of this we could also implement the use of smart assistants to carry out simple functions e.g.: Hey Siri, or ok google. The service of this product lies in the convenience factor, whereby vehicles can leave and enter a premise with a door opening as you arrive or close when you leave.

The hardware and software side of things can vary but there are some obvious choices available. A raspberry Pi is an ideal node on the wireless network as it is cheap and not much computing processing is required, other items required are a reed switch in which will detect the open or closed state of the door. Software side we can definitely side with either Homebridge.io or Hass.io.

Should the project be successful, the outcome will be an all in one plug and play solution to an extension of a smart home automation accessory which inevitably will make some lives a lot easier for motorists. With all the gizmos and gadgets out there already, this project will make it easier for general users who are not technically inclined to have a product that works from the box rather than a DIY solution.
